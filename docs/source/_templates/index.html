Getting Started with Trézor.io/start

Trézor.io/start is the official setup guide for Trézor hardware wallets, designed to help users securely store their cryptocurrencies. Whether you’re new to crypto or an experienced user, this guide ensures a smooth and phishing-free setup process.

Why Choose Trézor?
Trézor is one of the most trusted hardware wallets in the crypto space. It offers:

  • Top-notch security: Protects your private keys offline.
  • User-friendly interface: Easy to set up and use.
  • Wide compatibility: Supports over 1,000 cryptocurrencies.

Step-by-Step Guide to Trézor.io/start

  1. Visit Trézor.io/start: Always ensure you’re on the official Trézor website to avoid phishing scams.
  2. Connect Your Trézor Device: Use the provided USB cable to connect your Trézor to your computer.
  3. Install Trézor Suite: Download and install the Trézor Suite app for desktop or mobile.
  4. Set Up Your Wallet: Follow the on-screen instructions to create a new wallet or recover an existing one.
  5. Backup Your Recovery Seed: Write down your 12-24 word recovery seed and store it securely.
  6. Secure Your Device: Set a PIN for added protection.

Tips for a Secure Setup

  • Always double-check the URL to ensure you’re on the official Trézor website.
  • Never share your recovery seed or PIN with anyone.
  • Use a strong, unique password for your Trézor Suite account.
